Shannon Frantz

Father, Friend & Gamer

My friend Shanni is a legend, and on his 26th birthday, I'm reminded
how much he means to me. He's my best friend Beve's boyfriend, and I
used to take him for granted, thinking he'd always be there to queue
up for Overwatch, where he's a total beast, or to send me another
wild, racist Instagram reel we crack up over. You expect friends to
be around, hyping you to try new game strats or blasting Kendrick
Lamar's new album. He pushes me to step up, in gaming or appreciating
a sick rap verse, and it used to bug me. Now, I'm all in.

Our Korea trip this year was epic. Shanni was a pro at calling Ubers,
navigating Seoul's streets like a boss. From Olive Young to Gyeongbokgung,
he had it covered. Hearing him spit Kendrick's bars or geek out over Breaking
Bad plot twists is peak Shanni. I can't wait for him to finish Better Call
Saul so we can nerd out over Saul's schemes. When he DMs me racist or
transphobic reels, it means he’s still here, still Shanni.

Shanni's a gaming genius and he's annoyingly good. He knows wild stuff, like
optimal flank routes or how patches shift the meta. He drops these facts
without realizing how nerdy he sounds; he's just that into gaming. He breaks
down strategies like he's cooking up a Breaking Bad plan and carried me
through It Takes Two when no one else would, and I'm grateful. Our crew
knows he's a beast.

I used to be just Beve's friend, but now I'm Shanni's friend too, and our
bond's only gotten stronger. He expects everyone to bring their A-game, just
like he does. If you're slacking, he'll call you out - no mercy. That's how
he is, in games and life. It pushes me to level up, whether I want to or not.
I used to want to stay in the background, but Shanni makes me shine. He hypes
me up, whether I'm clutching a play or sending anti-poly reels. Not every
friend vibes like that. It's why he's Beve's boyfriend and my bro.

This birthday's huge because Shanni's been there through it all. He grinds
games, swaps reels, and keeps our Korea trip vibes alive. He deserves the best.
Shanni's about keeping it real for Beve and our crew, we want him to have a
day as awesome as he is. Happy 26th, Shanni - here's to more wins, reels,
and finishing Better Call Saul!

What a wonderful friend I have,
and it is his birthday!
